Product Information

A summary of the product

The SkyBites team has developed a product that will facilitate efficient delivery of food items in manual and automatic modes.
At the core of the project is the DJI RoboMaster TT Drone (shown left) which receives commands as specified in the TELLO Software Development Kit 2.0 to perform various operations.
To perform automatic food delivery of the drone, we have incorporated ESP-32 (shown right) chips that will assist with pathfinding and localization.
An ESP-32 chip will be attached to the head of the drone, serving as the anchor, while the remaining chips will be stationed at the various delivery locations.
